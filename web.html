done by human beings.This could include jobs like data entry,
analysis, and even some customer service positions.
While this may seem like a bad thing at first, it could actually
lead to new opportunities for people.
For example, if all data entry jobs are automated,
then companies will need people to oversee the machines
and make sure they are working properly. This could lead to
new job openings for people with different skillsets
